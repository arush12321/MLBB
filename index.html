MLBB HERO SHOWCASE

Minsithar

Minsithar is a fighter with strong crowd control and powerful late-game damage. He can melt tanks and eliminate squishy heroes easily. However, he can be countered by heavy CC heroes. He deals physical damage.

Johnson

Johnson is a tank/support hero. His ultimate allows him to transform into a car and carry a teammate. When he crashes into enemies, they take magic damage and get stunned for a short period of time. He deals magic damage.
